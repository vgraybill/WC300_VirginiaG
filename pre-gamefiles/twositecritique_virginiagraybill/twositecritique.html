Two Websites to Critique

Usability and Design Pros and Cons

Netflix

Popular TV and Movie Streaming Platform

Pros

  • Big, Bold hero video showcasing something new or popular to watch right away
  • Fully responsive with iterations for nearly all devices
  • Account details are easy to find, placed in the upper right hand corner
  • Finding extra details about the shows is intuitive
  • Clear usage of symbols such as a play button, thumbs up and arrows

Cons

  • Auto-playing a preview with sound whenever you hover over anything
  • Bogged down by too many choices on the initial load screen
  • Not enough spacing between rows of content making the site feel cluttered
  • The nav bar is extremely small and secondary at all sizes

Minecraft

Popular Open-world Sandbox Game

Pros

  • Call to action is prominantly displayed in the hero image, buy the game
  • Nav bar is fun and intuitive
  • Site stylings replicate the in game stylings, making it familiar to those already familiar with the game
  • Displays its products prominantly after the hero image

Cons

  • A few of the links link to outside affiliated sites but not back to the original site, such as the shop, support and education version pages.
  • Login is difficult to find quickly, small and crammed at the top
  • Theres a big white box on the top in the smaller versions of the website
  • While the social icons are fun and on theme with the rest of the website, they're also difficult to discern without a little extra thought

Full Course Homes in a Bite Sized Package...

Jim and Linda Jameson’s passion for tiny homes grew out of love for the enviroment, simplier living and a desire for freedom from the typical daily city grind.

Using Linda’s love and knowledge of architetural design, and Jim’s desire to build function living spaces, mainly the kitchen and entertaining areas, they designed their own tiny house in 2018.

After building their own tiny home, and helping a few friends realize their own dreams of smaller living, Jim and Linda decided to bring their unique designs to market for anyone to build. All you needed was the time, money and motivation.

These days, building a Bite Sized Home is easier than ever with their do it yourself build kits, 20+ affiliated contractors and furniture designs that fit any small space!
